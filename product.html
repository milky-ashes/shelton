Product Information

The Bellwether Light Grey is an end-on-end weave, a colored yarn is woven with a white yarn; appearing solid from a distance, revealing a more unique surface upon inspection.

  • 100 2/ply cotton end-on-end fabric
  • Fused collar (fusing gives rigidity to the garment)
  • Fused barrel cuffs with two buttons to adjust width
  • Reinforced placket with fusing
  • Hidden collar controls on point collar
  • No back pleat
  • Front pocket
