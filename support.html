Support

Making the product you design is hard.

Email is the easiest way to contact us and the best way to ensure you receive the most thorough answer. Please allow up to one business day for a reply. You have to lead a production team. You have to build a knowledge base. Maintain a factory full of machines. It’s hard.

All of it’s hard.

Most every brand I know doesn’t want to make their own product. Because it’s hard.

A brand can design clothing and then ask a factory to make it. That’s easy. But can they ever fully understand their art? We’ve been on both sides and we don’t think so.
